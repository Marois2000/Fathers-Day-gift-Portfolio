    Motorcycles

    As an avid motorcycle enthusiast, my passion for two wheels ignited at the young age of 23 when I built my first motorcycle—a '79 Harley Sportster—using milk crates and sheer determination. Since then, motorcycles have become an integral part of my life.


    Even today, I find immense joy in cruising on my bike and embarking on long-distance rides. One of my most memorable adventures was a ride to Sturgis, where I immersed myself in the vibrant motorcycle culture and forged unforgettable connections with fellow riders.


    Riding a motorcycle is more than just a hobby for me—it's a way of life. It symbolizes freedom, exhilaration, and a constant reminder to embrace the thrill of the open road. Whether I'm tinkering with my beloved Harley or exploring new horizons, my connection with motorcycles fuels my adventurous spirit and adds an extra dimension of excitement to my life.

    Exercising

    Fitness and working out are my true passions. Whether it's lifting weights, biking, or running, I wholeheartedly embrace the physical challenges that come with exercise. The feeling of pushing my limits and striving for personal growth drives me every day.


    One of my proudest achievements is building a home weight room with my son. Together, we transformed a dedicated space into a personalized gym, equipped with all the necessary equipment. This collaborative effort not only strengthened our bond but also reaffirmed our commitment to leading an active lifestyle. Engaging in regular exercise not only enhances my physical well-being but also improves my mental clarity. It empowers me to overcome obstacles and embrace new challenges, whether I'm pumping iron, hitting the trails on my bike, or lacing up my running shoes. Fitness is a vital part of my life, helping me take care of my body and mind.

    Having a Good Time

    Spending quality time with my loved ones is something I treasure deeply. Whether it's attending family gatherings, cheering on my nieces, nephews, and children at their sporting events, or simply enjoying the company of friends, these moments are invaluable to me.


    I thrive in social settings and have a knack for bringing joy and energy to any gathering. Parties come alive when I'm around, and I'm often recognized as the one who can light up the room. It brings me immense happiness to be surrounded by the laughter and warmth of family and friends.


    One of my favorite places to unwind and connect with others is the Elks Lodge in Nashua. It's a hub of camaraderie and community, where I can share stories, enjoy good company, and be a part of something larger than myself. At the lodge, you'll often find me at the center of the action, radiating positivity and being the life of the party.


    Creating lasting memories with my loved ones, supporting them in their pursuits, and fostering strong connections are the cornerstones of my life. I cherish every opportunity to be there for them and to create moments that will be remembered for a lifetime.
